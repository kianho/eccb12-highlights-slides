    sequencing.}
    %\framesubtitle{Dr.~Chris Sander, Sloan--Kettering Institute, USA}
    \emph{De novo} prediction of \beamerpurple{alpha-helical transmembrane

    \begin{itemize}
        \item No homologous structures, fragment libraries, or MD are used.
    \end{itemize}
